Recommended categories for Fighting Games
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHOOSHES --- Hand / Leg and Weapon movement through air

WEAPONS\Melee --- Hand weapons being wieled and hitting body/armor

THUDS & THUMPS --- Hits to body and body falls

GORE & SPLATS --- Blood and guts

FABRIC & CLOTHING --- Quick movement of clothes when punching / kicking

HUMAN\Grunts & Groans --- Vocalization when being hit and hurt

USER INTERFACES	--- User interface and menu naviation

BREAKS & SNAPS --- Bone breaking

VOICES\Male --- Announcer ("Fight", "You Win", etc.)

VOICES\Robotic Male --- Announcer ("Fight", "You Win", etc.)
